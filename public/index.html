About Me

I’m an aspiring full stack developer who is logic oriented with an eye for design. I am motivated by challenging situations, solving problems and looking for improvements to efficiency. I spent 10 years in the food industry the majority of which helping to run a small mom and pop butcher where I learned the importance of efficiency and tenacity. Currently I am most proficient at html, css, sass, js and jquery and am learning python.
